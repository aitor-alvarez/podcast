About

This website is a repository of language-learning podcasts. Finding podcasts that are suitable for or even relevant to language learning can be a challenge. The Community Podcast project presents and index of vetted podcasts that can be searched using parameters relevant to world languages, with the ultimate goal of exposing students to regional variations in language and cultural practices.
